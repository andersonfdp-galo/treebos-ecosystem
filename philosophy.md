# Philosophy 🧠

TREEBOS operates on a simple but strict sequence:

Perception → Reflection → Execution

---

## 1. Perception

Before acting, the system must understand reality.

Not assumptions.  
Not guesses.  
But the current, observable state.

---

## 2. Reflection

No action is taken without validation.

Information must be:

- Interpreted  
- Challenged  
- Confirmed  

Reflection is what prevents deviation, error, and illusion.

---

## 3. Execution

Only after perception and reflection does execution occur.

Execution is:

- Precise  
- Accountable  
- Traceable  

---

## Core Principle

> Acting fast is irrelevant if acting wrong.

---

TREEBOS prioritizes correctness, structure, and long-term reliability over speed or superficial results.

---

This philosophy governs all interactions between:

- Humans  
- Agents  
- Systems  

---

It is not optional.

It is foundational.
