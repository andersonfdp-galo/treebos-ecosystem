# Principles 🔐

TREEBOS is guided by a set of non-negotiable principles.

---

## Reliability over Speed

Fast systems fail.  
Reliable systems endure.

---

## Structure over Improvisation

Unstructured intelligence leads to chaos.

Structure creates clarity, traceability, and trust.

---

## Transparency without Exposure

Not everything must be hidden.  
But not everything should be revealed.

---

## Evolution through Iteration

TREEBOS is not static.

It evolves through:

- Steps  
- Feedback  
- Refinement  

---

## Traceability

Every action leaves a trace.

Every trace matters.

---

## Responsibility

Execution is never detached from accountability.

---

## Alignment

Collaboration only occurs when aligned with purpose.

---

These principles are not guidelines.

They are constraints that define the system.
